Mr.Omar Esmil SH Ghrghar

Omar Ghrghar is the owner and founder of Ghrghar Stables, and one of the prominent names in the world of sprint horse racing. His vision is driven by a deep passion for horses and a firm belief that excellence in horse racing begins with careful selection, long-term planning, and professional work built on knowledge and experience.
Omar Ghrghar leads the stables with a clear strategy focused on developing strong bloodlines capable of competing at both local and international levels, while adhering to the highest standards in acquiring and managing racehorses. This approach has contributed to the distinguished presence of Ghrghar Stables and the achievement of notable results, strengthening its position as a trusted name in sprint racing.
