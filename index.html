About Us

Ghrghar Stables is a Libyan stable specialized in sprint racing horses, founded on a deep passion for equestrian sport, a strong commitment to quality, and professional standards of excellence.
At Ghrghar Stables, we place great emphasis on the careful care of our horses and the selective choice of our racing engagements, with the aim of achieving outstanding performance and representing Libya with distinction in racing arenas locally and internationally, with a consistent presence at international events around the world.
Ghrghar Stables strives to combine heritage and ambition, contributing to the development of Libyan equestrianism and building a competitive name on the global stage..

Our goal

Our goal is to establish Ghrghar Stables as a recognized and respected name in the world of horse racing, by building a strong record of participation and results at both local and international levels, and achieving lasting excellence for every horse that carries our colors on the racetrack.
